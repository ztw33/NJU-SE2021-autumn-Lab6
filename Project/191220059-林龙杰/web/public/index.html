Frequently Asked Questions

Q:

How much does Primer cost?

A:

Nothing. Zero. Nada. Zilch. The app is totally free.

Q:

Who’s Primer for?

A:

Anyone looking to learn more about business and digital marketing, from small business owners to job seekers to brand managers.

Q:

What topics do you cover?

A:

We have lessons on social media, online advertising, web analytics, and storytelling, to name just a few.

Q:

How often will you update the app?

A:

We’re always adding new content and improving our product. If you'd like to hear about Primer updates, you can turn on our email notifications.

Q:

Who creates the lessons in Primer?

A:

Primer combines Google's knowledge with insights and expertise from leaders in the business and marketing industries, as well as other experts. Then we “Primerize” their insights to deliver them in the most accessible way possible.

Q:

I have an idea for a lesson. How can I get involved?

A:

We’d love to hear what’s on your mind. Send us a message at support@dworks.io.
